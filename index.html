Biography

I am currently a third-year PhD studen in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
My research interests focus on Computer Vsion, especially diffusion model, auto-regressive model, image generation, low-level vision tasks, and Fourier theory, advised by Feng Zhao and Feng Wu.
